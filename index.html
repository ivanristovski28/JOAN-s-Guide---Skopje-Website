About Joan Apartments

More Than a Stay —
A Local Experience

We are a family-run business built on trust, hard work, and genuine care for every guest who stays with us. Joan Apartments was created with one simple goal: to offer comfort, warmth, and a place where you feel welcome from the moment you arrive.

We enjoy discovering new places, good food, adventure, and everything that makes a city feel alive. That is why we created this guide — to share our favorite cafés, restaurants, hidden spots, nightlife, and experiences Skopje has to offer.

Whether you are here for one night or a longer stay, we want you to feel at home and enjoy the city like a local.

  • Family-owned with personal care
  • Comfortable modern apartments
  • Local recommendations you can trust
  • Fast support whenever needed
